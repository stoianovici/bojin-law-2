Final Decision

Approach B (Compact) with contextual actions:

  • Removed: View toggle (list/bubble) - not needed
  • Uncategorized emails: Show "Atribuie la dosar" + "Contact personal", no privacy toggle
  • Assigned emails: Show "Reasignează" + privacy icon (owner only), no "Contact personal"
  • ⋮ menu: Deschide în Outlook, Email nou
