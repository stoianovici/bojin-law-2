Case No. 2025-CV-1234

IN THE CIRCUIT COURT OF COOK COUNTY, ILLINOIS

COUNTY DEPARTMENT - LAW DIVISION

JOHN SMITH,

Plaintiff,

v.

JOHNSON CORPORATION,

Defendant.

DEFENDANT'S MOTION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT

NOW COMES Defendant Johnson Corporation, by and through its attorneys, and hereby moves this Honorable Court for entry of summary judgment in its favor and against Plaintiff John Smith on all counts of Plaintiff's Complaint, and in support thereof states as follows:

I. INTRODUCTION

This case arises from Plaintiff's allegations of breach of contract and negligence relating to a commercial transaction between the parties. As demonstrated below, there are no genuine issues of material fact and Defendant is entitled to judgment as a matter of law on all claims.

II. STATEMENT OF UNDISPUTED FACTS

1. On January 15, 2024, Plaintiff and Defendant entered into a written agreement for the purchase of commercial goods (the "Agreement").

2. The Agreement contained a limitation of liability clause limiting damages to the purchase price of goods.

3. On March 1, 2024, Defendant delivered the goods pursuant to the Agreement.

4. Plaintiff accepted delivery and paid the agreed-upon purchase price of $50,000.
