# PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

Effective Date: January 15, 2024

PARTIES:
Client: Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation
Provider: TechConsult LLC, a California limited liability company

1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to provide software development consulting services for Client's mobile application project.

2. TERM
This Agreement commences January 15, 2024 and continues through June 30, 2024.

3. COMPENSATION
$150/hour, invoiced bi-weekly. Payment due within 15 days.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product shall be owned by Client upon full payment. Provider retains rights to pre-existing materials and may use generic techniques and know-how.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidential information for 3 years. Standard exclusions apply.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice. Client pays for work completed through termination date.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Provider's liability shall not exceed fees paid in the 6 months preceding the claim.

8. GOVERNING LAW
State of Delaware

IDENTIFIED ISSUES FOR TESTING:
- Hourly rate structure needs expense reimbursement clause
- IP ownership clear but missing warranty provisions
- Confidentiality period shorter than industry standard (typically 5 years)
- No indemnification clause for IP infringement
- Missing dispute resolution mechanism

RISK LEVEL: MEDIUM
COMPLEXITY: Moderate - standard service agreement with minor gaps
