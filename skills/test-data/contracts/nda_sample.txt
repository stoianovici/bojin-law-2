NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (Test Sample)

This NDA is between TechCorp Inc. and InnoSoft Solutions, effective March 1, 2024.

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION: Business plans, source code, customer lists, financial data.

2. OBLIGATIONS: Both parties shall maintain confidentiality, not disclose to third parties, use only for business discussions regarding potential partnership.

3. TERM: This agreement shall remain in effect for two (2) years.

4. EXCLUSIONS: Information already public, independently developed, or received from third parties without confidentiality obligations.

5. RETURN OF MATERIALS: Upon termination, all materials must be returned or destroyed.

6. REMEDIES: Breach may result in injunctive relief in addition to other remedies.

7. GOVERNING LAW: State of California

---
TEST CRITERIA:
- Expected clauses identified: confidentiality, term, exclusions, remedies, governing law, return
- Risk level: LOW (standard terms)
- Missing: Indemnification clause, dispute resolution
- Quality: ⭐⭐⭐ (basic but functional)
