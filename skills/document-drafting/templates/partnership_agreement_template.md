## JURISDICTION-SPECIFIC NOTES

**Delaware:**
- Consider forming Delaware LP/LLP for flexibility and favorable case law
- File Certificate of Limited Partnership with Secretary of State
- Revised Uniform Partnership Act (RUPA) governs
- Strong asset protection for limited partners

**California:**
- Register with California Secretary of State and Franchise Tax Board
- File Form 568 (LLP/LP) and pay annual fees ($800 minimum)
- California Corporations Code governs partnerships
- Community property considerations for married partners

**New York:**
- File Certificate of Partnership with Department of State
- Register for NY sales tax if selling taxable goods/services
- Partnership Law Article 8 (general) or Article 8-A (limited) applies
- Consider LLC instead of partnership for liability protection

**Texas:**
- File with Texas Secretary of State (Form 701 for LP)
- No state income tax but franchise tax applies
- Texas Business Organizations Code governs
- Annual report due date: May 15

**UK:**
- Register with HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) for Self Assessment
- Partnership Act 1890 governs unless modified by agreement
- Partners taxed individually on profit share (no partnership-level tax)
- Consider Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) for liability protection
- File annual confirmation statement with Companies House (LLP only)
